Human Psychology and
Real World Effect

There is no denying that humans are smart creatures, however, sometimes we can be decieved by our own
bodies. Social media is able to take advantage of this contributing to the spreading of fake news.

Humans long to fit in and be part of a group, and social media sites allow this to happen with features that
allow us to people with similar interests to connect and talk. When you get a group of people like this together
it becomes much more likely that there will be bias in what stories get shared. This bias can become a problem
because there will be no one questioning what is being read and no one determining whether or not what you
are seeing is fake news or not.

The results of this can end up becoming dangerous too, one TEDEd discuessed both how fake news spreads
and gave a great example of how this had dangerous effects, mainly with a result of people not getting their kids
vaccinated. That can be found here.
