Social Medias Impact on Fake News







Mission statement

This website will give a brief summary of some of the ways social media platforms are able to distribute fake news and why we are likely to believe it.

Overview

Every single day we go on social websites and scroll through our feed and with every scroll we are being infulenced to believe fake news. Fake news itself is any story that is meant to deliberately give us false information, and is often done through unethical journalism.

By the time you finish reading all the information on this site you should be able to have a better understanding of fake news so you can become a more informed user who won't fall victim to the fake news traps most people do.
