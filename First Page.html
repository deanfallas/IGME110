Social Media Bots

Social Media Bots are becoming far too common as time goes on. Before I get started explaining how bots are effective, I should explain that a social media bot is essentially a coded social media account that will automatically and continuously complete specified tasks (How Is Fake News Spread? Bots, People like You, Trolls, and Microtargeting, Center for Information Technology and Society - UC Santa Barbara).

With that being said social media bots are incredibly effective at spreading fake news for a couple reasons:

  • They are undectable by humans
  • They target people who will retweet what they say and will make it seen by the most people
  • They find fake news stories before most people know about them so it will be more believable.
These bots are incredibly difficult to detect by humans, but if you know what to look for, then it will be possible to avoid a decent amount of it. The Pew Research Center has a great video on identifying twitter bots, (which can be accessed by clicking the photo below).
